StarLink and SriLanka

As of now we all know that the current world is emerging towards extreme technologies, Within that recently American entrepreneur and billionaire Elon musk, his company SpaceX has Contributed much when compared to others. Recently SpaceX’s mass project called star link was launched only on selected regions which is still under beta testing. Starlink is a satellite internet constellation constructed by space x.

Why is it better?
Within US context it would not be fair to say that the us telecons have failed to provide high quality telecommunications services in recent years. But star link in the other hand has lower latency rate due to its low orbit circulation. Also multiple packages for various needs make the network more efficient. Currently 5g network is on implementation which provides the internet speed in Gbps. 4g is a ground base network where as 5g a sky base network. More satellites on sky ,stronger omnipresent 5g is. So we all gets the question, why Starlink, better we to go with 5g?, this is where both of this technologies split path. Significance of 5g is to provide better network to urban areas, where Star link’s main objective is to provide highspeed internet to remote-areas.

Sri Lanka on star link
Telecommunication regulatory commission of Sri Lanka(TRCSL) announced that they are under discussion with Starlink on initiating the network in the near future. “it was a very positive discussion We will continue to have technical deliberation as the next step .this is a private investment and the TRC is setting up the required environment for them to come here ,”says (TRC)director general Oshada Senenayake.

Will it replace the current service providers?
The director general clearly mentioned that Starlink would not compete with the existing service providers and that it is introduced for specific cases.
