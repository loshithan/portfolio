Frustrated on choosing a correct laptop for programming?

Nowadays its is mandatory to have laptops regardless what field of study you follow. But as a computer science student we know the agony of a slow computer. Even though coding doesn’t require a hi spec machine, cs students are not constrained to just coding, to use Autodesk maya, adobe packages for their projects it requires a quite amount of specification for a linear work flow. So here are some tips for you to choose a correct machine for your need.

Screen
As of now there are LCD, IPS, OLED screens available in market. it is better to spend on a good screen Cause as a cs student you have to spend your time looking at the screen for several minutes. Touchscreen on the other hand is not a key factor for a programming laptop. it is mostly required for Designing purposes. Also refreshing rates really matters if you want to experience a smooth workflow you should choose a refresh rate above 60hz.


Ram
Ram on the other hand also plays major role in your workflow. greater ram capacity faster your program runs also it enhance your multitasking ability. In current situation it is mandatory to have at least 8gb of ram to cope up with heavy tasks.


Processor
In market the popular competitors are intel and AMD .we are not going to dig deeper on both of this topics but you need at least i5 5th gen or equivalent processor to experience burdenless process.


GPU
GPU work simultaneously with CPU to manipulate the computer graphics. Nvidia ,MSI and intel are some of the companies which offer better GPUs. Unless you are a gamer or a designer you would not need a hi end GPU. for programming intel’s integrated graphics is more than enough.


Operating system
As we all know , there three major providers macOS ,Microsoft and Linux. But it is a decision of yours to go for either of them. If you prefer macOS you definitely want a MacBook otherwise a windows based laptops is suitable for Linux and windows OSes.
